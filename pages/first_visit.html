Your First Waxing Experience

Welcome to Daria Aesthetics!

We're excited to guide you through your first waxing appointment. While it's natural to feel a bit nervous, proper preparation and knowing what to expect will help ensure a comfortable, positive experience. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know!

How to Prepare Before Your Appointment

Hair Length Requirements:

Stop shaving 2-3 weeks before your appointment: Your hair needs to be at least ¼ inch long (about the size of a grain of rice) for the wax to grip properly. If hair is too short, it cannot be removed effectively. If you haven't shaved in a while and hair is longer than ½ inch, you can carefully trim it down, but don't go too short!
Avoid tweezing or plucking: Only shaving should be stopped before waxing. Don't tweeze, thread, or use other hair removal methods that remove hair from the root, as this disrupts the growth cycle.

24-48 Hours Before:

Gently exfoliate the area: Use a mild scrub or exfoliating glove to remove dead skin cells. This helps the wax adhere better and allows for a cleaner removal. Don't over-exfoliate or irritate the skin.
Moisturize your skin: Keep your skin hydrated with a light, oil-free moisturizer. Well-hydrated skin experiences less discomfort during waxing. However, avoid heavy creams or oils that can create a barrier.

Important Medication Information:

If you're using any of the following products, please inform us before your appointment:

  • Retinol, Retin-A, or Accutane: These products increase cell turnover and thin the skin, which can lead to skin lifting or tearing during waxing.
  • Other prescription topicals: Certain medications can make your skin more sensitive.
  • Blood thinners or aspirin: These can increase sensitivity and bruising.

Please let us know about any skin sensitivities, allergies, or medical conditions during your consultation.

Day of Your Appointment:

Shower and cleanse thoroughly: Make sure the area to be waxed is clean and free of oils, lotions, sweat, makeup, or deodorant. Clean skin is essential for effective waxing and hygiene.
Skip the lotions and oils: Don't apply any products to the area that will be waxed on the day of your appointment. These create a barrier that prevents the wax from adhering properly.
Wear loose, comfortable clothing: Choose breathable, loose-fitting clothes made from natural fibers like cotton. This is especially important for bikini, Brazilian, or underarm waxing. Your skin will be sensitive after the treatment, and tight clothing can cause irritation.
Consider taking a pain reliever: If you're concerned about discomfort, you may take an over-the-counter pain reliever (like Tylenol or Ibuprofen) about 30-60 minutes before your appointment. However, avoid aspirin as it can thin the blood.

Timing Your Appointment:

  • Avoid scheduling during your menstrual cycle: Your skin is more sensitive during this time, which can increase discomfort. Try to schedule your appointment between cycles if possible.
  • Plan ahead for special events: If you're waxing for a vacation, wedding, or special occasion, schedule your appointment 2-3 days in advance. This gives any redness or irritation time to subside.

What to Expect During Your Appointment

Warm welcome and consultation: When you arrive, we'll greet you and guide you to a private treatment room. We'll discuss your preferences, any concerns you have, your skin type, and review your medical history. Feel free to ask any questions! Communication is key to ensuring your comfort.
Privacy and comfort: Depending on the area being waxed, you may need to partially undress. We provide complete privacy and will ensure you feel comfortable throughout the process. For bikini and Brazilian waxes, you'll undress from the waist down. We remain professional and focused on providing the best service.
Skin preparation: We'll cleanse the area to remove any remaining oils, lotions, or debris. For certain waxing methods, we may apply a pre-wax oil or powder to protect your skin and ensure optimal results.
The waxing process: Wax is applied in small sections and then quickly removed, pulling hair from the root. We work efficiently to minimize discomfort while ensuring thorough hair removal. The process typically takes:
  • Small areas (brows, upper lip): 5-10 minutes
  • Medium areas (underarms, bikini): 15-20 minutes
  • Large areas (legs, Brazilian): 30-60 minutes
Post-treatment care: After waxing, we'll apply a soothing product to calm your skin and reduce any redness. We'll also provide personalized aftercare instructions and answer any questions you have.

Managing Discomfort

Let's be honest: waxing can be uncomfortable, especially the first time. However, most clients find it's much more manageable than they expected. Here's what you should know:

It gets easier: Your first wax may be the most uncomfortable because hair is thicker and the follicles are stronger. With regular waxing (every 3-4 weeks), hair grows back softer, finer, and less dense, making each subsequent appointment more comfortable. Many clients report that their third wax is significantly easier!
Everyone's tolerance is different: Pain is subjective. What feels uncomfortable to one person may be barely noticeable to another. We work at a pace that's comfortable for you and can take breaks if needed.
Breathing helps: Deep, slow breathing can significantly reduce discomfort. Try to relax your muscles rather than tensing up, which can make things more painful.
Communication is key: If you're feeling too much discomfort, let us know! We can adjust our technique, take breaks, or provide additional comfort measures.

After Your First Wax

Expect some redness and sensitivity: It's completely normal to experience pinkness, slight swelling, or sensitivity for a few hours after your first wax. This should subside within 24 hours. If redness persists beyond 24 hours or you notice unusual reactions, please contact us.
Follow aftercare instructions: Proper aftercare is essential! Avoid heat, sun, swimming, exercise, and touching the area for 24-48 hours. Apply soothing products like aloe vera gel. For complete aftercare instructions, please visit our Waxing Aftercare Page.
Schedule your next appointment: For best results and to maintain smooth skin, plan to return every 3-4 weeks. Regular waxing keeps your hair growth cycle synchronized and makes the process progressively more comfortable.

Common First-Timer Questions

Will it hurt?

The level of discomfort varies by individual and the area being waxed. Most people find the anticipation worse than the actual experience. The first wax may be more uncomfortable, but it becomes easier with regular appointments as hair grows back finer.

How long will the results last?

Waxing results typically last 3-6 weeks, depending on your individual hair growth cycle. Hair grows back softer and finer than when you shave.

Will I get ingrown hairs?

Ingrown hairs can occur, but proper exfoliation and moisturizing between appointments significantly reduces this risk. We'll provide you with tips to prevent ingrown hairs.

What if I have sensitive skin?

Please inform us about any skin sensitivities during your consultation. We can recommend specific products and techniques for sensitive skin and will perform a patch test if needed.

Can I wax during my period?

While it's possible, we recommend scheduling your appointment between cycles when your skin is less sensitive. If you must wax during your period (for bikini/Brazilian services), please wear a fresh tampon or menstrual cup.

What if my hair is uneven?

It's completely normal for hair growth to be uneven during your first wax, especially if you've been shaving. The growth will even out after 2-3 consistent waxing appointments.

Tips for the Best First Experience:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water before and after your appointment.
  • Try to relax! Anxiety can heighten discomfort. Listen to music, practice deep breathing, or chat with your esthetician.
  • Don't judge waxing by your first experience. Give it 2-3 sessions before deciding if it's right for you.
  • Trust the professionals. We've performed thousands of waxing services and will guide you through the process with care and expertise.
  • Reward yourself afterward! Treat yourself to something nice after your appointment.
