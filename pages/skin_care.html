Waxing Aftercare Instructions

Thank you for choosing Daria Aesthetics!

Proper aftercare is essential to maintain smooth, healthy skin and get the best results from your waxing treatment. Please follow these guidelines carefully to prevent irritation and ensure optimal healing.

Immediately After Your Wax (First 24 Hours)

What TO DO:

Apply a soothing product: Use aloe vera gel or a post-wax lotion to calm and hydrate your skin. These products help reduce redness and provide a cooling sensation.
Keep the area clean: Gently cleanse the waxed area with a mild, fragrance-free cleanser and lukewarm water. Pat dry with a clean towel.
Use a cool compress: If you experience discomfort, apply a cool compress or ice pack wrapped in a clean cloth for 10-15 minutes to reduce inflammation and soothe the skin.
Wear loose, comfortable clothing: Choose breathable, cotton fabrics that won't rub against or irritate your freshly waxed skin. Avoid tight-fitting clothes, especially for bikini or Brazilian waxes.

What NOT to do:

Avoid These for 24-48 Hours:

  • No hot water: Avoid hot showers, baths, hot tubs, saunas, and steam rooms. Heat can increase irritation and open pores, leading to potential infection.
  • No sun exposure or tanning: Your skin is extra sensitive after waxing. Stay out of direct sunlight and avoid tanning beds. If you must go outside, apply a broad-spectrum sunscreen.
  • No swimming: Avoid pools, oceans, lakes, and hot tubs. Chlorine, salt water, and bacteria can irritate freshly waxed skin.
  • No exercise or sweating: Skip the gym, cardio workouts, and any activities that cause excessive sweating. Sweat can irritate the area and clog newly opened pores.
  • No touching: Keep your hands away from the waxed area to prevent introducing bacteria and oils that could cause breakouts or infection.
  • No makeup or heavy products: For facial waxing (brows, upper lip), avoid applying makeup, self-tanners, tinted moisturizers, or products with harsh chemicals or fragrances.
  • No exfoliating products: Your skin has already been exfoliated by the waxing process. Avoid scrubs, loofahs, or harsh cleansers for the first 24-48 hours.

Days 2-7 After Your Wax

Begin gentle exfoliation (after 48 hours): Starting 2-3 days after your wax, gently exfoliate the waxed area 2-3 times per week. This helps prevent ingrown hairs by removing dead skin cells and allowing hair to grow freely. Use a gentle sugar scrub or exfoliating mitt designed for sensitive skin.
Moisturize daily: Keep your skin hydrated by applying a lightweight, oil-free, fragrance-free moisturizer every day. Well-moisturized skin is less prone to irritation and ingrown hairs. Avoid heavy oils or products that can clog pores.
Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water to help your skin heal and maintain smoothness from the inside out.

Ongoing Care Between Waxing Appointments

Maintain your exfoliation routine: Continue exfoliating 2-3 times per week to prevent ingrown hairs and keep skin smooth.
Keep moisturizing: Daily moisturization is key to maintaining healthy, smooth skin.
Don't tweeze between appointments: Resist the urge to pluck stray hairs! Tweezing disrupts the hair growth cycle and can lead to uneven results at your next waxing appointment.
Schedule regular appointments: For best results, wax every 3-4 weeks. Consistent waxing makes hair grow back softer, finer, and less dense over time, making each treatment more comfortable.

Pro Tips for Best Results:

  • Your third wax is typically your best wax! Hair growth cycles even out, making the experience more comfortable.
  • Allow hair to grow for 3-4 weeks between waxing appointments for optimal results.
  • If you experience any unusual redness, swelling, or signs of infection that don't subside within 24 hours, please contact us or consult your healthcare provider.
  • Natural, cotton-based fabrics are your best friend after waxing!

Special Considerations

For facial waxing (brows, upper lip): Be especially gentle with your skin. Avoid touching the area with your fingers, which can introduce oil and impurities. Wait at least 24 hours before applying makeup or using active skincare ingredients like retinoids or exfoliating acids.
For bikini and Brazilian waxes: Wear fresh, breathable cotton underwear. Change out of wet bathing suits promptly. The area may feel more sensitive for the first treatment, but sensitivity typically decreases with regular waxing.
For body waxing (legs, arms, back, chest): Wear loose clothing immediately after treatment. Avoid abrasive materials that might irritate the skin.
